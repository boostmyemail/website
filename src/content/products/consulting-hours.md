Sometimes you don't need a full engagement — you need a senior person to think through a specific problem with you for an hour or two. That's what this is.

Consulting hours are sold in blocks. You book the time, we meet, and you walk away with clarity on whatever you brought to the table.

## What we can cover

Consulting sessions work best for focused, bounded questions. Common uses:

- **Strategy review** — you've got a plan, you want a second opinion from someone who's run these programs before
- **Deliverability troubleshooting** — open rates dropped, you're not sure why, you want a systematic diagnosis
- **Sequence or campaign review** — you're about to launch something and want a trained eye on the copy, structure, and logic
- **Platform questions** — HubSpot or Customer.io configuration, workflow architecture, segmentation logic
- **Hiring and team questions** — you're building an in-house email function and want to think through the role, the hire, or the stack

If you're not sure whether your question fits, book a call anyway. Worst case, I'll tell you it's better suited to a different format.
